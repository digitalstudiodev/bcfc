Our Mission

Through innovative programming and community engagement, we break down barriers and bridge the gap between diverse talent and employers. Our commitment to technical skills, education, and safety ensures a thriving and equitable environment where all individuals, regardless of background, can succeed and contribute to the growth of the NJ/NY production sector. Together, we are a catalyst for positive change, providing opportunities for all and fostering a more inclusive and vibrant creative community.

By upholding these principles, we strive to encourage underrepresented groups to enter the thriving and safe film industry, thereby contributing to the growth of the NJ/NY production sector.

Brick City Film Center is not just a school; it is a force for positive change and a beacon of hope in the world of film production. Together, we will work towards generating greater interest, opportunities, and jobs for underrepresented groups in the film and television industry.
